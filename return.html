🔄 Bite & Bills - Return & Refund Policy

We care about our customers! Here’s how our return & refund process works.

🍔 Wrong Order Delivered

If we delivered the wrong item, we’ll replace it with the correct one at no extra cost.

⏱ Late Delivery

If your order is delayed beyond 60 minutes, you can request a refund or free voucher.

❌ Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 10 minutes of placing it. After that, food prep begins.

💳 Refund Method

Refunds will be processed within 3–5 business days via your original payment method.
